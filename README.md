# Advanced Computer Network and Cloud Computing 

## Course Synopsis
This course delves into the intricate and advanced aspects of computer networking. It encompasses a comprehensive study of IPv6‚Äôs technical intricacies, the revolutionary concept of Software-Defined Networking (SDN), and the practical implementation of various networks including wireless, sensor-based, Ad-hoc, and the cutting-edge 5G network.

In the subsequent part of the course, students are presented with a unique opportunity to conduct research in these areas and beyond, extending into the realm of cloud computing. This hands-on experience fosters a deeper understanding and practical application of the concepts learned, thereby equipping students with the skills necessary to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of computer networking.

## Course Learning Outcomes
1. Describe the state-of-the-art technologies in computer networking.
2. Evaluate the demands of cloud computing in various domain of applications.
3. Agree among a team the solution in a current needs and¬† problems of computer networks.
4. Present effectively the issues regarding computer networking technologies in the challenging environments.
